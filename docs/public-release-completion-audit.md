## Current stopping conditions

Build 67 cannot be called a fully verified public release candidate until it is fully physically
accepted; the consolidated iPhone tests and performance/stability evidence are complete; the exact
Devin-account retention treatment is confirmed; the frozen policy is published and byte-verified;
App Store privacy, age, Content Rights, and commercial declarations are complete; private review
credentials and final screenshots are installed in App Store Connect; and explicit App Review
submission approval is recorded. Developer ID signing, Apple notarization, and Connector publication
are complete. A separate fresh-account Connector lifecycle exercise remains a hardening follow-up.
Build-spec targets such as the seven-day crash-free window and timed OTA rollback drill remain real
gates unless the specification is explicitly changed.

## Current stopping conditions

Build 65 cannot be called a fully verified public release candidate until Apple processing completes, the consolidated iPhone/iPad tests, performance/stability evidence, exact Devin-account retention confirmation, the frozen policy is published and byte-verified, App Store privacy/age/Content Rights/commercial declarations are complete, Developer ID/notarization and clean-account Connector checks pass, private review credentials and final screenshots are installed in App Store Connect, and explicit submission approval is recorded. Build-spec targets such as the seven-day crash-free window and timed OTA rollback drill remain real gates unless the specification is explicitly changed.
